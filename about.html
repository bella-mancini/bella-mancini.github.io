Spectrum

During my 2024 summer internship with Spectrum I interned as a Product Management Intern on the Mobile Product Team. This internship gave me insight in what it's like to work in a fourtune 500 company and what goes into creating a customer facing self service platform with 32 million users. I engaged in several projects by creating Business Requirements documents for tracking and to highlight the importance and outcomes of the projects as well as the project tickets in Jira and their associated user stories, Epics, and Lucid charts. In my lucid charts I summarized the problem, ways to solve it and made low fidelity mockup to quickly familiarize the people with the project and for documentation during the design kickoffs I ran. I was also able to do backwards sprint planning and was invested in decision making for what tickets to include or holdoff for future sprints. Overall, my biggest takeaway from this internship was how big companies collaborate and ensure the product is the most reliable it can be for customer satisfaction.

Covestro

For the summer of 2023 I interned at Covestro as a Technology and Digitializtion intern on in their HR department. Working in a global companies HR department's internal technology team gave me a better insight into how companies are run and the technology that supports it. I created back to front end code (Python) and used Microsoft the Power Apps tool to push out 8 apps throughout the summer. These apps where requested by HR Business Partners and other employees to stream line processes and where in a variety of areas from compensation to employee relations. Each of these apps included automated workflows (PowerAuntomate) and created data achetecture repositories (PostgresSQL/ Sharepoint) to support applications. To create these apps I used the iterative design process by meeting with stakeholders to collect requirements, crafting wireframes (Figma), and supporting senior teammates in implementatiion, testing, and deployment.
