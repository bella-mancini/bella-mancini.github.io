Professional

As far as professional experience, I have had customer service experience since I was in high school in the food industry and retail which gave me a great understanding of how to work with people and develop a great work ethic.

The summer of 2023 I was given the amazing oppurtunity to work with a company named Covestro as a HR Technology and Digitializtion intern. Working in a global companies HR department's internal technology team gave me a better insight into how companies are run and the technology that supports it. I created back to front end code (Python) and used Microsoft the Power Apps tool to push out 8 apps throughout the summer. These apps where requested by HR Business Partners and other employees to stream line processes and where in a variety of areas from compensation to employee relations. Each of these apps included automated workflows (PowerAuntomate) and created data achetecture repositories (PostgresSQL/ Sharepoint) to support applications. To create these apps I used the iterative design process by meeting with stakeholders to collect requirements, crafting wireframes (Figma), and supporting senior teammates in implementatiion, testing, and deployment.


In the summer of 2024 I had my second internship with Spectrum as a Product Management Intern on the Mobile Product Team. This internship gave me insight in what it's like to work in a fourtune 500 company and what goes into creating a customer facing self service platform with 32 million users. I immediately got hands on experience through attending meetings shadowing my mentor on her projects as well as having 4 projects of my own. In these projects I created Business Requirements documents for tracking and to highlight the importance and outcomes of the projects. I also was in charge of creating the project tickets in Jira and their associated user stories, Epics, and Lucid charts. In my lucid charts I summarized the problem, ways to solve it and made low fidelity mockup to quickly familiarize the team with the project and use during my meetings with the design team to kickstart ideation. I was also able to do backwards sprint planning and was invested in decision making for work to include in sprints and what to hold off for future sprints. Overall, my biggest takeaway from this internship was how big companies collaborate and ensure the product is the most reliable it can be for customer satisfaction.
