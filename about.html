Professional

As far as professional experience, I have had customer service experience since I was in high school in the food industry and retail which gave me a great understanding of how to work with people and develop a great work ethic.

The summer of 2023 I was given the amazing oppurtunity to work with a company named Covestro as a HR Technology and Digitializtion intern. Working in a global companies HR department's internal technology team gave me a better insight into how companies are run and the technology that supports it. I created back to front end code (Python) and used Microsoft the Power Apps tool to push out 8 apps throughout the summer. These apps where requested by HR Business Partners and other employees to stream line processes and where in a variety of areas from compensation to employee relations. Each of these apps included automated workflows (PowerAuntomate) and created data achetecture repositories (PostgresSQL/ Sharepoint) to support applications. To create these apps I used the iterative design process by meeting with stakeholders to collect requirements, crafting wireframes (Figma), and supporting senior teammates in implementatiion, testing, and deployment.
