Denver Zoo re-design

Introduction

summary

This case study is a redesign the Denver Zoo's ticket purchasing flow. The current flow was confusing, had too many screens, and overall was not a user friendly experience. Their homepage also had information overload and it was difficult to understand what was important.

Role

I conducted this study myself.
