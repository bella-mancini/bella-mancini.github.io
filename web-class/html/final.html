Ride The Bus

Rules

there are 4 rounds in this game. In order to win you must guess correctly on each round in a row. If you guess wrong at any point you must drink (either a non-acoholic or acoholic) beverage and start again from round one. You may get lucky and get off the bus or ride it all night until you give up.
